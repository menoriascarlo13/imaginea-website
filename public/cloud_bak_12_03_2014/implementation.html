Implementing XaaS

We have deep expertise in setting up IaaS & PaaS environments spanning multiple datacenters spread out geographically.

Our custom build scripts for IaaS help auto-scaling, self-service and high availability to span across datacenters, significantly reduces infrastructure expenses, operations overhead and makes critical IT resources available for strategically important projects.

We help build PaaS environments that are readily suited for migrating existing applications into a cloud environment and leveraging all the cloud benefits. We have expertise in setting up all major database, middleware and application components on cloud to host 3-tier, 2-tier and single tier applications quickly and with minimal customization needs.

On-Premise to Cloud migration

Migrating existing applications to cloud requires in-depth understanding of both on-premise and cloud environments. Our legacy in developing middleware technologies, provides us the unique advantage of understanding the internals of IT infrastructure, and as a basis for application performance improvement.

Imaginea is the partner you need to migrate critical business applications from on-premise servers to private/hybrid/public cloud infrastructure while maintaining the performance, scalability and control objectives.

Special Purpose Cloud (for Big Data, Mobility, Analytics, Secure Data)

Cloud environments can be tuned for specific business requirements. For example, running a twitter feed analytics application requires a strong networking component in the cloud to handle the high velocity feeds. Imaginea can help you build these special purpose cloud environments.

Our deep expertise in tuning network, hardware & middleware components gives us the capability to build special purpose clouds tuned to meet the unique performance, availability, security and scalability needs of applications.

Automating DevOps increases the business value of cloud eco-systems. From auto-scaling across cloud environments, to self-services, to connecting the cloud to monitoring tools are significantly enhanced through DevOps automation.

Imaginea adopts an agile methodology for DevOps automation. This ensures that benefits from DevOps automations are realized much faster and meet your unique business needs.

Contact us for our DevOps white paper - and check out the use case from one of our success stories.

A DR site has been a huge cost burden on IT organizations. It continues an unavoidable risk mitigation measure against an unforeseen disaster. Cloud offers an "Insurance premium" model to this enterprise risk, reducing the upfront costs significantly and improving its business value. DR on cloud can be configured to be either a warm or hot site based on the business priorities.

We can help build DR sites that suit your organizations specific requirements leveraging our expertise across private/hybrid/public cloud technologies.
