Cloud Security Assessment: Any compromise to the security of your cloud solution can put mission-critical business operations at risk. Also impacted are the users and potentially any sensitive data. Your cloud's security posture needs to be assessed, evaluated, to understand the effectiveness, identify gaps and take corrective measures.. Imaginea's cloud security assessment is designed to help you gain an in-depth understanding of the current state of your cloud solution's security and protection. We will measure your cloud security against industry best practices and any mitigration needs.

Cloud Security Access : You need safe access to your on-premise and cloud applications with a industry standard cloud-based identity and access management solution to negate any potential business risks and compliance challenges. Imaginea consultants can deliver identity management for your on-prem and cloud based applications.

By proactively testing your cloud deployment against real-world threats, you are assured of protecting your company's reputation, projecting your customer data, and preserving customer trust. Irrespective of who your IaaS provider is, you are responsible for maintaining the requisite layers of security. As your deployments grow over time, it becomes all the more important to test and tune your applications. A tune-up of your cloud environment protects you, saves you time and also leads to enhanced performance of your applications.
