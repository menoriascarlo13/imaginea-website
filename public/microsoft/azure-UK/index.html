Powering enterprises
with azure

There's something that cloud evangelists don't tell you - the platform only matters as much as you know your way around it. Cloud can also limit your business for lack of expertise and direction.

You are here

You're depending on physical infrastructure which is starting to become a problem as you scale and face unpredictable demands for resources.

You're not one for exaggerated hand waving, but you're lost. You've decided to go team cloud, but you're not sure what your options are or how you can use them best.

Where do you go from here?

Imaginea can help you make key decisions with expert, hands-on consultation. We'll actively iron out roadblocks, demo new features and help you set up, run and maintain your cloud operations.

Sparking a Better Cloud Strategy

Provision and Scale Storage

Cloud storage and database management is a brilliant move for organizations who want to avoid making disproportionately large investments for in-house servers, but still have definite plans to scale business.

Should you go for a G-series VM or Premium Storage with a lesser VM? Make storage choices that make sense for your business and set it up in a trice with Imaginea.

We can also help you create and manage relational or NoSQL databases, or connect to already existing ones. And instead of overprovisioning for that sudden spike in demand, choose a flexible database pool with our Azure database expertise.

Develop and Orchestrate Apps

Software development has deeper implications that Imaginea can help you plumb.

Build web, mobile, logic and API apps with Imaginea's comprehensive Focus solutions. Focus abstracts the best practices for the cloud, and leverages analytics and Azure's app, mobile, media and RemoteApp services. Now you can find the right cloud direction for your business without getting bogged down with the details.

Having trouble building and running cloud services at scale? We'll guide you through the Service Fabric to simplify support for stateful microservices.

And Imaginea's open source expertise will make it easier for you to add third-party services and use apps, frameworks and languages that you're familiar with while working with Azure.

Explore Cutting-edge technologies

If your business is on the vanguard of current technologies, you'll find that Imaginea's expertise extends to Azure's newer offerings as well, whether it's IoT or Docker containerization. Nitro - Imaginea's MS lab, is dedicated to constantly searching for newer and better ways to leverage the cloud.

We're excited by modern developments including predictive analysis with Azure ML, containerization runtimes like runC and cross-platform development with Xamarin.

But how can you toe the thin line between modern technology and bleeding-edge solutions? Our technical guidance includes building PoCs, and comprehensive benchmarking and comparison testing. We're fully invested in your success.

The Bottom-line

Why maintain outdated applications when you can strategically modernize and migrate them in the best possible way for your organisation?
