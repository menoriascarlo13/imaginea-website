About Imaginea Cloud Services

Imaginea Cloud Services vision is to help our enterprise customers implement cloud (XaaS) services. Our services offering is designed to deliver a full range of Cloud Lifecycle Management solutions that allows our customers to focus on their core business initiatives, benefits and protects them from ever-changing security threats and mandated compliances.

Imaginea's experience working with our customers from verticals like healthcare, insurance, retail, financials ensures that we have the specific expertise needed to execute complex projects successfully. With our Imaginea Cloud Services team, we bring our enterprise software heitage and history in product development; ability to build a quality lasting products. Furthermore, Imaginea's Cloud Competency Center provides our customers an opportunity to run a pilot with their specific use case and get educated on the latest cloud technology.

We are actively collaborating with key industrial partners to continuously enhance commercial portfolio so our customers can get first feel of cloud technology.

Choosing how to effectively embrace cloud computing is one of the most important decisions you will make in your organization. We therefore invite you to visit us, so that you can experience first-hand what cloud computing technology can do for you..
