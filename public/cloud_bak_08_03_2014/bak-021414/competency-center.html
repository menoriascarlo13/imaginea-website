Imaginea Competency Center

We have state of the art Cloud Competency Centres that are geo located across the globe, Mountain View in California & Hyderabad, Bangalore and Chennai, India. This is a state of the art cloud innovation and technology center in industry that provides customers with an opportunity to experience the benefits and reliability of cloud-based IT infrastructure.

This centre is geared for the mutual benefit of our clients, business partners. Its role is to support continuous improvement and optimization of cloud lifecycle management solutions, foster innovative thinking to fully harness the potential of cloud computing.

The centre is staffed by highly skilled specialists from Imaginea's technical team, all with extensive hands-on experience in implementing cloud solutions.

The competency centre provides education to our customer and partners through webinars, configuration recommendations, implementation and methodology guidelines, technical white papers and reports.

Showcasing Demos

Running customer use cases

Running bench marks

Hosting environment for tech support

Educating customers
