Build, Scale, Succeed with Imaginea

The mobility landscape is moving at warp speed. EMM vendors are adding new features every three months. Simple device management solutions are morphing into enterprise mobility suites with capabilities moving beyond device management to content management, application provisioning and audit, and policy and service management.

As the race to dominate a market - expected to hit $175 billion in 2017 - heats up, vendors and their engineering teams are under intense pressure and face multiple challenges:

Scaling - solutions must work across very large number of corporate mobile users

Platform Diversity - multiple devices and interfaces, in a BYOD world - and new ones from Amazon, Microsoft, others

Quality Assurance - design, automation and execution of testing across multiple environments
