Organizations today have more information than they can handle. Mountains of it. As enterprises demand better, simpler solutions - for the vendors catering to this fast growing market - it often means more complexity, and cost. The issues facing engineering teams are:


Scalability: Handle increasing data needs, work across devices and support hundreds of thousands of users in multiple geographies

Emerging trends: Disk-based backup appliances with deduplication are simple, but flash solid state storage appliance are the trend. Storage as a Service is a new and emerging cloud technology and Cloud Storage Gateway is also becoming a key component of a cloud-based solutions - the any-to-any transfer of data

Risk Management: Growing connectivity, greater storage management access, the need for disaster recovery, data privacy and compliance have heightened wareness of the vulnerability of data solutions

Quality Assurance: Design, automation and execution of testing across multiple environments


In-house engineering teams are under pressure to focus on the core product and continual innovation but qualified engineers with the right software expertise and knowledge of regulated industries and compliance issues are hard to find, let alone attract.
