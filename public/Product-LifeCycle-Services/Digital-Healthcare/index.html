Imaginea's Mobile Healthcare Apps

See. Buy. Eat.

An entrepreneur in the healthy snacking business wanted a mobile presence for an existing product. The design goal was to have users get a visual sense - the next best thing to smell - of the snack or food before they add it to their box.

Need now: a personal trainer

Two fitness entrepreneurs in the Valley saw an an opportunity to create a meeting place for personal trainers with spare time and people seeking personal attention and advice. The design goal was to help both of them discover each other quickly and easily. It also helps people identify the right trainer for their needs.

Take charge of your health

A challenging app developed for a health information entrepreneur. The requirements and market segmentation were vague and fast moving which meant that the design was constantly evolving. The design goal was to make personal health information easily accessible in a way that made sense.
