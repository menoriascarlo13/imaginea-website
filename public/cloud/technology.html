Cloud presents a variety of problems and solutions; Multi tenancy, storage options, deployment models, service models etc. Making a choice that works for your organization can get tricky. Imaginea experts conduct detailed business and technical analysis of the existing technology landscape in your organization and recommend a powerful cloud solution. Our tested reference architecture will aid in quick and neat provisioning. Imaginea consulants can define an enterprise cloud strategy and adoption roadmap building custom architectures with public, private and hybrid clouds.

Organizations look for business value from Big Data. Along with business evaluation it is imperative to evaluate the IT infrastructure readiness to handle big data initiatives. Our services can help you define the functionalities and capabilities necessary to align your IT with your Big Data projects.

We assess your IT environment and work with you to build an integrated IT strategy that supports the collection, consolidation, management, and security of your information. We provide you with an IT architecture that's appropriate to your Big Data goals; to manage the volume, velocity, variety and value of your data.

Bank on our experience to set up an Hadoop Ecosystem and integrate with cloud storage using OpenStack and Savanna.

Deploying and migrating to cloud is only the start of our cloud movement. You may be deploying private, public, or hybrid cloud, the fundamentals of systems management and monitoring do not change. Cloud operations management requires unified visibility and real-time awareness of the entire cloud infrastructure. It is imperative to conduct some up front planning, understand the level of operational awareness that is needed by the business, and rolling that into the design.

A cloud is elastic only to an extent; but the shrinkage and expansion has to be well thought out, designed and even anticipated. How do you anticipate your business loads? When do you expect capacity to be available and when is it maxed out? Do you have an understanding of business spikes? Are you ready to handle them? How much of available capacity is currently being consumed? What is the efficiency of your deployment?

Get these answers and more with the Imagine Capacity Sizing engagement.
