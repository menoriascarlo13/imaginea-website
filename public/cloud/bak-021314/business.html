One of the first steps towards launching a cloud initiative is for the enterprise to run through a comprehensive financial planning and analysis. To determine the ROI of any cloud project, we have to take into account a variety of factors. An enterprise might see infrastructure provisioning as the most important consideration, another might have DevOps optimization as the paramount need, while others may be concerned about industry compliance mandates.

The Imaginea consulting team can help your enterprise understand and optimize your infrastructure services and other cloud services to maximize profitability and ROI. We can analyze your business needs and translate the concepts into financial models, understand current deployments towards a comprehensive cloud resources utilization model. We can analyze your subscription options, resource utilization, billing etc. Taking into considerations present and future growth expectations, desired financial SLAs, and industry trends, our consultants can make the right recommendation. Let us make recommendations and help you take the first few steps towards success in the world of cloud.

Imaginea Cloud Services consultants view CLOUD as the "lean philosophy" for datacenters with benefits... Through our engagement, we help organizations drive cost savings, and IT agility by transforming IT infrastructure and adopting leaner IT service management.

We can help you with the following:

Cloud Business Case & Roadmap

Cloud Business Case & Roadmap: Imaginea consultants can build a comprehensive cloud roadmap that maps with your core business strategies, initiatives and challenges. Seeking options? A staggered approach into the world of cloud? Imaginea can deliver!

Cloud enabled IT Processes:

With the advent of cloud technology, businesses have a new way of marketing, communicating, innovating, educating and collaboration. IT department are becoming an on-demand services (ITaaS) that is providing intelligent services to their customers, and in an on-demand fashion, agnostic of device.

Are you ready to derive that speed and agility, along with savings, and high customer sat? Think ITaaS! ITaaS implies self-configured, self-healing system that reacts with changing business needs, increasing or decreasing sales. Create your own ITaaS by engaging our Imaginea consultants. Make that shift from maintenance focus to innovation focus.

Organization Change Management:

Leveraging technology for business advantage just does not succeed without addressing the human dimension. Are you meeting the expectations of your employees, their concerns? Are you training your IT staff to handle the new world of cloud? Do they understand the compliance mandates of your industry, and scenarios where cloud is an absolute no, no? Do you have a firm handle over the cloud licensing model as against the traditional licensing? Imaginea Consultants can help your enterprise plan for these changes, get over the fears, and lead to that successful transformation.

A vital aspect of adopting cloud is knowing how well your existing infrastructure and applications adapt to the cloud. Are there certain risks you need to mitigate prior to making that leap? Our consulting team helps you with that assessment.
