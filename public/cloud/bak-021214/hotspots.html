Cloud Provisioning

After finalizing the company's cloud computing strategy, next biggest task is provisioning. We help you to provision your cloud with following key goals in mind


Reduce IT costs and complexity by enabling self-service requests and automating operations

Accelerate application deployments using repeatable workload patterns



Cloud provisioning also entails developing the processes for interfacing with the cloud applications and services along with auditing and monitoring.

Managing your cloud

Visibility, Management and IT service assurance are key characteristics; deploying and migrating to cloud is beginning of our cloud movement. You may be deploying private, public, or hybrid cloud, the fundamentals of systems management and monitoring do not change. Cloud operations management requires unified visibility and real-time awareness of the entire cloud infrastructure.



We analyse and propose to you following 3A's action plan to keep watch on Cloud Infrastructure.

Awareness: What needs to be collected and monitored

Analytics: How and what need to be analysed

Action: What action you need to take



Major Deliverables to you include detailed specifications of infrastructure to be monitored, various tools be used to keep a watch on the cloud. Deliverables also include bill of materials, cost estimates, implementation plan and a complete mapping of monitoring technologies and various partner if any.

DevOps Optimization

Your company is under increasing pressure to respond quickly to changing business environments?

Your IT department is under pressure to support this changing business conditions?

Integrated approach of Cloud and automated configuration system is the key to meet changing business needs



This responsiveness requires organizations to be agile and underlying infrastructure which can provide this agility is cloud. It is only obvious to leverage cloud computing to meet the time-to-market needs of business. So organizations are looking for automation and cloud computing to go together, to help in quickly provisioning the applications. This integrated approach is the DevOps.

Imaginea experts will sit, discuss and analyse your IT infrastructure. They will look at how the automation can be applied to your current configuration system. Based on all this we will help you create private cloud with automated provision using the tools like configuration tools like Puppet and Chef.



We will help you define the cloud strategy, design, implementation and automating your configuration process.
